# Product

## Register

product

## Users

TGMS is used by board-facing executive, legal, compliance, risk, audit, cyber, privacy and AI governance teams working in a regulated insurance group context. Primary users need to scan readiness, route work to the correct owner module, review legal or compliance posture, and execute real prototype flows where the data contract is already connected.

## Product Purpose

TGMS Platform is an advanced governance prototype for Grupo ARGA Seguros. It composes several owner modules without collapsing their responsibilities: Secretaría Societaria owns formal corporate acts and document generation, GRC Compass owns risks and compliance workflows, AI Governance owns AI systems and AI-specific evaluations/incidents, and TGMS Console composes readiness, search and handoffs.

Success means the product is credible for executive review, honest about demo versus productive posture, and navigable as a working prototype without hidden schema assumptions.

## Brand Personality

Expert, restrained, institutional. The interface should project legal rigor, operational confidence and calm command of complex governance workflows.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a generic SaaS landing page, decorative AI dashboard, card-heavy marketing mockup, or playful consumer app. Avoid glassmorphism, gradient text, decorative blobs, oversized hero metrics, fake evidence finality, vague "AI magic" copy, and any UI that hides module ownership or suggests TGMS mutates owner data.

## Design Principles

- Owner-first clarity: every screen should make source of truth, module ownership and mutation posture easy to understand.
- Executive scanability: dense, structured information should be readable quickly by senior stakeholders.
- Legal honesty: demo, HOLD, read-only and productive states must never be visually blurred.
- Prototype realism: connected flows should feel operable, while unconnected areas should say so plainly.
- Garrigues discipline: module UI must follow the green Garrigues token system and avoid native Tailwind color shortcuts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA for contrast, keyboard navigation, visible focus, labeled controls, semantic structure and responsive behavior. Motion should be restrained and should not depend on layout-shifting animation.
